Full Body Every Other Day

Full Body Every Other Day

Level: All

Hits every muscle 3+ times weekly with built-in recovery days between sessions. Maximizes MPS frequency (Schoenfeld et al.) without exceeding systemic recovery capacity. Ideal for natural lifters who respond to higher frequency.
Upper / Lower

Upper / Lower

Level: Inter+

Two structural variants: ULURULR biases the upper body (3 upper / 2 lower per week) for lifters whose lower body recovers faster or who want torso emphasis. ULR uses balanced 2/2 frequency with recovery built-in.
PPL / Upper-Lower Hybrid

PPL / Upper-Lower Hybrid

Level: Adv

An advanced 5-day fusion: Push / Pull / Legs followed by Upper / Lower. You get the volume distribution benefits of PPL plus a second hit on the torso and lower body via UL — all within one week. Excellent for advanced lifters seeking high volume with smart recovery.
